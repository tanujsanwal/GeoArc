Industry applications

  • Water

    GIS is a powerful tool for water, storm water and wastewater management.

  • Remote Sensing

    GIS is a powerful tool for change detection and DEM extraction.

  • Photogrammetry & Imaging

    GIS is a powerful tool for Aerial Photography, Orthophoto creation and 3D terrian model/ Surface modelling.

  • In-Building Facility Management

    GIS is a powerful tool for managing inside building.

  • Airports

    With GIS, Airport operators can collate complex data, allowing more effi cient planning and operations.
